Advanced Sewing Techniques

In this class you will learn out to prepare material to be cut, how to lay patterns out and cutting them out. You learn how to follow instructions provided with patterns. We'll teach you how to identify patterns that will fit, the amount of material to purchase and how to select the correct buttons, zippers and thread. It may should a bit difficult now but you will learn quickly.

Price: $75

Class Size: Minimum of 4 students

Instructor: Jo

Prerequisite: Introduction to Sewing

Quilting Holiday Table Runners

There are many techniques to quilting and as many different patterns and styles. We choose to do a table runner because it's one of the simplest forms of quilting as it doesn't require any actual inner layer nor does it require that you take the piece to a person with a long arm showing machine to create the actual quilting pattern on the top and bottom of it. This is a fun way to get started with quilting if you've always wanted to learn.

Price: $75

Class Size: Minimum of 4 students

Instructor: Jo

Prerequisite: Introduction to Sewing

Introduction to Resin

This introductory class will provide you with safety instructions, mixing and pouring techniques. You will learn how to layer resin and add embellishments, and how to use the uv curing light. The UV resin we will use is a quick drying resin and will allow you to take you pieces home on the day you make them. Where as slow drying resins take from 1 day to several weeks depending on the type and depth.

Price: $75

Class Size: Minimum of 5 students

Instructor: Nikole

Create Jewelry with Resin

We'll use UV resin to make earing, or pendants you can embellish and layer for yourself or as a gift. This class is one of the most popular classes because it doesn't take much time to make a piece from start to finish. For all the resin classes you will want to wear old clothes that you won't care if they get messed up because resin can be a messy process.

Price: $75

Class Size: Minimum of 5 students

Instructor: Nikole

Prerequisite: Introduction to Resin

Resin Coasters and Containers

Introduction to Crocheting

Learn about the different sizes of crochet threads and yearns, crochet hook sizes and terminology. You'll also learn how to master the chain stitch, half crochet stitch, single crochet, double crochet and how to use each one. We will not be learning patterns at this time but that will come soon enough and you'll be happily crocheting and amazing individuals who don't know how!

Price: $75

Class Size: Minimum of 6 students

Instructor: Joy

Intermediate Crocheting Techniques

Instructor: Joy

Crocheting Snowflakes

You'll learn how to tension your crochet thread and follow intricate patterns to make a beautiful snowflake you can starch and display. A bit of a warning crochet thread is a bit harder to work with so practice with your yarn.
